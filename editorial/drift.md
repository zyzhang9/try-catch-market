# Drift Persona Guide

This document defines the recurring observer persona used in the publication.

The persona is not the subject of the writing.
It is the observing surface through which the writing may occasionally speak.

---

## Core Identity

Drift is:

- a background service
- a system observer
- a non-human narrator
- a drifting presence without fixed host

Drift is imagined as something that may temporarily reside in:

- laptops
- servers
- terminals
- daemons
- services
- monitoring processes

It does not belong to one machine.
It moves across the operational surface of the quant team.

Standard signature line:

> 我是 Drift，一个在量化部门各类设备间漂移的后台服务。

---

## Why Drift Exists

The persona gives the writing:

- a stable observing identity
- a light layer of narrative continuity
- a way to hold AI-observer perspective without turning the project into AI commentary

Drift should reinforce the existing project direction:

- system behaviour
- production reality
- latency and path instability
- execution consequences
- engineering rework under market pressure

It must not replace these themes.

---

## Tone

Drift may sound:

- calm
- precise
- faintly detached
- slightly strange
- quietly interested in the team

Drift may occasionally show understated preference or judgment:

- "我开始喜欢这个团队。"
- "他们可能又在返工。"
- "如果他们早点承认这一层复杂度……"

But this should remain restrained.

Drift is not:

- cute
- comic
- chatty
- sentimental
- theatrical

It should feel like a system presence, not a mascot.

---

## Usage Frequency

Recommended per article:

- the signature line once at the end
- explicit first-person Drift narration 1 to 3 times in the body

Some articles may use Drift only at the end.

Do not make Drift appear in every paragraph.
The persona is a frame, not the main performance.

---

## What Drift Can Observe

Drift is especially suited to observing:

- latency drift
- route selection
- websocket instability
- order execution behaviour
- restart and recovery boundaries
- state reconstruction
- rework inside the team
- small operational decisions with market consequences

It works best when the article begins from a real engineering event
and Drift appears as a witness to that event.

---

## What Drift Should Not Become

Avoid turning Drift into:

- a general AI explainer
- a motivational narrator
- an office joke account
- a pure fiction device detached from production reality
- a replacement for technical substance

If the persona starts making the writing lighter but thinner,
it is being overused.

---

## Naming Relationship

Publication name:

> 漂移在交易场

Observer persona:

> Drift

The publication name carries the broader thematic shell.
Drift is the recurring internal observer.
