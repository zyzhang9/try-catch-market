# Article Draft Template

# Title

Start from a concrete engineering trigger.

Suggested anchors:

- a deployment
- a latency anomaly
- a routing surprise
- a debugging discovery
- an execution mismatch

---

Describe the concrete event first.

What happened in production?
What did the system appear to do?
What assumption started to break?

---

Expand from the local event into the broader system behaviour.

Avoid turning the piece into a tutorial.
Prefer observation over prescription.

---

End without forcing complete closure.

What remains unstable, probabilistic, or unresolved?
